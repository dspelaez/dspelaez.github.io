About me



My research involves the analysis of in-situ and remote sensing ocean wave data as well as the use of numerical models to better understand wave dynamycs. I am particularly fascinated by the intricate role of ocean waves in energy transfer, momentum exchange, and mass fluxes between the ocean and the atmosphere.
